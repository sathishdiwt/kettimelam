What "Kettimelam" means

Kettimelam is the traditional nadaswaram-and-thavil orchestra that has opened Tamil weddings for generations — played at the muhurtham, the kalyana procession and every auspicious arati. We took our name from that sound because it's exactly what we promise: a wedding rooted in tradition, delivered without a single detail missed.

Our Story

A Perambalur team that plans weddings the way your grandparents would recognise

Kettimelam A to Z Wedding Planner started as a family Kettimelam band serving villages around Perambalur, and grew — event by event — into a full wedding and public-function management team. Today we still play the melam ourselves, and we still show up a day early to check every chair, light and garland by hand.

Whether it's a two-day village kalyanam, a hall wedding in Trichy, or a temple festival for the whole panchayat, our promise is the same: one point of contact, one team, every service covered — genuinely A to Z.

Questions

Frequently asked questions

Does Kettimelam handle both weddings and public events?

Yes. Alongside full wedding planning, we manage temple festivals, political meetings, corporate launches and other public functions across Perambalur district.

What is a Kettimelam band?

Kettimelam is the traditional Tamil wedding orchestra of nadaswaram and thavil that announces auspicious moments — the muhurtham, kalyanam procession and arati — with live percussion and wind music.

Which areas do you serve?

We're based in Perambalur and regularly travel to Tiruchirappalli, Ariyalur, Thanjavur and Pudukkottai districts.

Can we book only one service, like décor or catering?

Absolutely — every service can be booked individually, or combined into our full A-to-Z package at a better overall rate.

How far in advance should we book?

For the wedding season (Nov–Feb), we recommend booking at least 3 months ahead so your preferred band and décor dates are secured.
